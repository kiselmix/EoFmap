A New World

Hunt down 1 Skinny Dogs. They appear at any time of the day.

A Place to Call Home

Locate and destroy 3 Giant Scorpions. They appear at any time of the day.

Equivalent Exchange

Reggie Mae needs to find a metal pipe among the scrap metal. Look for the pipe in the crates on the map.

Helix Institute Calls For Aid

Find and retrieve the Quantum Beacon from RAIDER RANCH.
Reward: 9 MEDUSAL STEAM

Gift For The Verdant

Recover 3 Phased Cells from the INFECTED OUTPOST.
Reward: 16 GUN POWDER

Creepy Crawly

Find and deliver 3 Lice Cells from CLIFFSIDE TOWN Go to CLIFFSIDE TOWN (cross the bridge and go all the way to the left) and look for 3 Lice Cells.
Reward: 19 COPPER

New Type Of Mutation

Bring 5 Red Cells located in HILDEN CITY.
Reward: 11 MEDUSAL STEAM

For Science!

Go to the SERMON ROSTRUM location and look for three Spore Samples.
Reward: 7 TRANSISTOR

Prep Phase

Go to the BRIDGE CHECKPOINT location and look for 2 Access Keys

Identity Crisis

Go to the MISSILE SILO location and look for 12 ID Badges

Breaking And Entering

Go to the GAS STATION location and look for 5 Access Codes

Humble Beginnings

A man named Reggie May wants you to kill Skinny Dog. They appear at any time of the day.

Stuck In Here With Them

Find and defeat 3 Giant Scorpions. They appear at any time of the day.

Clean-Up Crew

Kill 25 Gray Wasps. Day, Evening and Night appear.
Reward: 15 QUARTZ

Breach And Clear

Eliminate 20 Heavy Rollers. Morning, Day and Evening appear.

Bug Hunt

Destroy 25 Boomer Spiders. They appear at any time of the day.

Stuck In Here With Me

Go to the SCRAP YARD location and kill the Juggernaut boss. Day and Evening appear with a 50% chance, Night with a 100% chance.
Reward: 22 MEDUSAL STEAM

The Helix Research Institute

Locate and bring back 3 Research Journal in The Raider Ranch.
Reward: MEDUSAL CELL STAGE I

Investigating Cannibalism

Locate and bring back 4 Metal Tablet in Cliffside City.
Reward: NOMAD ARMOR

The Blood God

Investigate The Infected Outpost and find a Strange Polaroid.
Reward: MEDUSAL CELL STAGE I

A Strange Monster

Locate and destroy 30 Underground Spitters. Morning and Day appear.
Reward: DUSTY SCATTERGUN

One Man's Treasure

Reach old man's house, North of Hillden City.
Reward: 4 GEAR

The Echelon of Eternity

Destroy 25 Gray Wasps. Day, Evening and Night appear.
Reward: MEDUSAL CELL STAGE I

Blood Rituals

Find 5 Blood Vials in the Hillden City.
Reward: 12 MEDUSAL STEAM

The Lost Idol

Find a Rust Totem from Sermon Rostrum.
Reward: 5 MERCURY

Finding Proof

Find 5 Tapes from the Missile Silo.
Reward: 14 MEDUSAL STEAM

A Decryption Quest

Find a Tape Decoder in the Missile Silo.
Reward: 10 ALUMINUM

Supplies

Find 3 Acid. Acid is obtained only from items! You can see which items contain Acid in the Recycling tab.
Reward: 40 STEEL

Descendants of The Blood God

Destroy 100 Boom Spiders. They appear at any time of the day.
Reward: 17 MEDUSAL STEAM

The Failed Experiment

Locate and bring back 1 Beast Claw in Sermon Rostrum.
Reward: 16 OIL

The Apex Predator

Go to the SCRAP YARD location and kill the Scorpion Monarch boss. Day and Evening appear with a 50% chance, Morning with a 100% chance.
Reward: 20 MEDUSAL STEAM

A Study of Cells

Bring back 1 Medusal Cell Stage II. Medusal Cell Stage II can be crafted at the Bio Station (terminal on the top right), for this you need 100 Medusal Stem.
Reward: 100 MEDUSAL STEAM

Meeting The Council

Find a Hard Drive from the Missile Silo.
Reward: 14 QUARTZ

Favours of The Council

Find a Raider Trophy from the Sermon Rostrum.
Reward: 22 MEDUSAL STEAM

Suspicious Activity

Destroy 35 Rooks. Morning and Night appear.
Reward: 19 COPPER

The Hidden God

Go to the SCRAP YARD location and kill the Juggernaut boss. Day and Evening appear with a 50% chance, Night with a 100% chance.
Reward: 22 MEDUSAL STEAM

A Little Job

Locate and bring back 1 Satellite Dish.
Reward: 21 RUBBER

Raider Trouble

Find a Barbed Wire in Bridge Checkpoint, Raider Ranch.
Reward: 18 BUTANE

Abandoned Outpost

Find a Walkie Talkie from the Infected Outpost.
Reward: 2 TRANSISTOR

Pile of Rust

Find a Old Radio from Hillden City.
Reward: 23 MEDUSAL STEAM

The Fight Pit

Go to the Scrap Yard and find a Jewelry Box.
Reward: 1 GOLD

Alien Cells

Find a Pulsating Cell from the Gas Station. They can also be found on the Fairwater map.
Reward: 25 MEDUSAL STEAM

Goin' on a Hunt

Destroy 25 Thirstmites.
Reward: 28 MEDUSAL STEAM

The Sacred Scrawl

Investigate priest's hut. The house near the "THE CHURCH" is marked in yellow on the map. Search the bookshelf.
Reward: 5 MERCURY

Otherwordly Deities

Locate and bring back 1 Lost Effigy in The Church Area.
Reward: 6 TITANIUM

Fairwater

Destroy 7 Blastcaps
Reward: MEDUSAL CELL STAGE I

Mutagenic Life

Go out and find 4 Fairwater Mold Samples from the Trailer Park.
Reward: 13 GLASS FIBER

Deep Mutation

Locate and bring back 3 Swamp Mold in the Roadside Cafe area.
Reward: 11 MEDUSAL STEAM

Heathens

Locate and bring back 3 Religious Writings in The Church area.
Reward: 14 GLASS FIBER

What's Unique 'bout Moonshine?

Locate and bring back 1 Moonshine in Abandoned Manor.
Reward: 1 TITANIUM

Connections

Destroy 30 Carrion Hulks.
Reward: 24 MEDUSAL STEAM

One Ring To Rule Them All

Find a Signet Ring from Fairwater's Casino.
Reward: 33 GLASS FIBER

To The Rhythm Of The Beat

Investigate melomane's hut and find a vinyl record.
Reward: 4 TRANSISTOR

Clean-up Mission

Destroy 55 Carrion Hogs.
Reward: 2 KEVLAR

Telepathic Comms

Locate and bring back 1 Muscle Tissue in the Old Barnyard.
Reward: 1 GOLD

New Horizons

Reach the heart of the infection on the far North of Fairwater.
Reward: AUTO RIFLE TURRET

Rabid Critters

Destroy 50 Rotten Stalkers.
Reward: 25 MEDUSAL STEAM

Blighted Harvest

Find 50 PolyBulbs. Look in the red zone at the very top of the map.
Reward: 3 URANIUM

The Hive Mind

Destroy 100 Poison Runners.
Reward: 7 ACID

Coded Crop

Find 10 Gene Fruits. Search the Cliffside map for boxes with medicinal plants.
Reward: 1 EXTRA STRONG PAINKILLERS

Trial By Fury

Destroy 70 Mawtoucheds. Extremely rare to encounter, lives near the boss's lair on the left-top of the map.

Aim For The Eye!

Kill Nightmare Goliath (boss) in the far north side of Fairwater. There are two identical bosses on the map, you can kill either one.
Reward: 25 MEDUSAL STEAM

The Idols

Locate and bring back 3 Metal Idol from Riverboat Casino.
Reward: 24 MEDUSAL STEAM

Lost Samples

Find 6 Jitter Bones from the Fishing Huts.
Reward: 14 WOOD

Pest Control

Destroy 50 Poison Runnerss.
Reward: 1 FATIGUE BANDAGE

Killing Spree

Destroy 70 Carrion Matriarchs.
